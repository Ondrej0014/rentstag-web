THE MAINTENANCE NETWORK

Maintenance without the Markup.

Most agencies treat repairs as a revenue stream, adding 10-20% on top of every invoice. We don't. You pay exactly what the vetted contractor charges. Zero markup. Zero conflict of interest.

Get Access

Vetted & Tested

We don't Google for plumbers. We use a closed network of contractors we've worked with for years. If they fail our quality standards, they are out.

Guaranteed Speed

Our vendors agree to strict SLAs. Emergency? They are there in 2 hours. Routine fix? Scheduled within 48 hours. No ghosting.

Wholesale Pricing

Because we give them volume work, we negotiate rates you couldn't get as an individual owner. We pass those savings directly to you.

The "Invoice = Invoice" Policy

In standard property management, a €100 repair often becomes a €120 charge to the owner. The agency pockets the difference. At Rentstag, a €100 invoice is charged as €100. We make money from management fees, not from your misfortune.
